Ocean Wave Pouch

A textured pouch inspired by ocean waves, perfect for small essentials

Description

This charming pouch captures the beauty and movement of ocean waves through intricate wave stitch crochet. Made with soft, durable 100% cotton yarn, it’s the perfect size for holding your daily essentials - from cosmetics and jewelry to coins and keys. The textured wave pattern not only looks stunning but also provides a lovely tactile experience.

Materials

  • 100% cotton yarn (medium weight)
  • Size G/6 (4mm) crochet hook
  • Tapestry needle for weaving in ends

Dimensions

  • Length: 16 cm (6.3 inches)
  • Height: 12 cm (4.7 inches)
  • Width at base: 10 cm (4 inches)

Care Instructions

  • Machine wash cold, gentle cycle
  • Lay flat to dry
  • Reshape while damp if needed
  • Avoid bleach and fabric softeners
