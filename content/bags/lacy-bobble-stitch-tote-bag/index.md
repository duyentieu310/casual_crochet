## Lacy Bobble Stitch Crochet Tote Bag

I came across a beautiful bobble bag pattern by <a href="https://vivcrochets.com/crochet-bobble-bag-free-pattern-video-tutorial/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Viv Crochets</a>, and it immediately made me want to try making one of my own.

The original pattern uses a solid bobble stitch, but I wondered what it would look like if the fabric was a little lighter and airier. So instead of following it exactly, I improvised and experimented with a lacy bobble stitch. The result is a crochet tote bag that keeps all the lovely texture of bobbles while feeling much more open and perfect for warmer days.

I really enjoy projects like this, where I can start with an idea and see where it takes me. Sometimes a small change in stitch completely changes the personality of a bag.

Made with 100% cotton yarn, this tote feels soft yet sturdy, and the open texture gives it a relaxed summer look. I can already imagine taking it to a weekend market, the beach, or simply carrying it around on a sunny afternoon.

## Details

- 100% cotton yarn
- 5 mm crochet hook
- Lacy Bobble Stitch
- Lightweight crochet tote bag
