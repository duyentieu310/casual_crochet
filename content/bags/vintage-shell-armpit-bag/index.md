## Vintage Shell Stitch Crochet Bag

Sometimes I like to imagine finding a handmade crochet bag tucked away in a little vintage shop.

That feeling inspired this project. The shell stitch naturally creates soft, layered textures that remind me of traditional crochet, so I paired it with muted lavender and marled cream yarn to give the bag a timeless look.

My favorite detail is the strap. Instead of leaving it plain, I added gentle ruffles along both sides, giving the bag a unique finish that makes it feel a little more special.

Made with 100% cotton yarn, this crochet shoulder bag has enough structure to hold its shape while still feeling soft and comfortable to carry.

## Details

- 100% cotton yarn
- 4 mm crochet hook
- Shell Stitch
- Bag size: approximately 21 × 16 cm
- Height with strap: approximately 40 cm
