## Vintage Shell Bag

For this project, I wanted to make something that felt like it had been found in a little vintage shop.

The shell stitch was the obvious choice. It has that soft, layered texture that reminds me of old crochet pieces passed down through generations. To lean into that feeling, I paired muted lavender with a marled cream yarn. The colors aren't loud, but together they give the bag a gentle, worn-in charm.

My favorite part, though, has to be the straps. Instead of keeping them plain, I added little ruffles along the edges. They're subtle, but they completely change the personality of the bag. It feels a little playful without taking away from the vintage look I was aiming for.

Like most of my projects, this one is made with 100% cotton yarn. It has enough structure to hold its shape while still feeling soft in your hands. Seeing everything come together—from the shell pattern to the textured straps—made this one especially satisfying to finish.

## Details

- 100% cotton yarn
- 4 mm crochet hook
- Shell stitch
- Bag size: approximately 21 × 16 cm
- Total height with strap: approximately 40 cm
