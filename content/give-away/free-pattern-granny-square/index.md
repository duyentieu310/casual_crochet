## Striped Lemon Peel Bag


There wasn't a pattern or a grand idea behind this one. I simply wanted to play with colors and see how they looked together. The Lemon Peel Stitch gave the stripes just enough texture to keep things interesting while still letting the colors do most of the talking.

I enjoyed making it but probably won't use it myself. I'd love for someone else to finish it and make it their own. Add a zipper, a magnetic snap, a button, or even a lining, there are lots of ways to give it a second life. Feel free to send me photos of the finished project by [email](mailto:tieuduyen310@gmail.com). I'd love to see how it turns out! 😊
